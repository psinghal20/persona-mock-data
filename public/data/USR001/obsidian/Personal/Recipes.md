## Quick Weeknight Dinners

### Honey Garlic Salmon
*Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 4*

A simple, flavorful salmon dish that comes together in under 30 minutes.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each, skin-on, wild-caught preferred)
- 4 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce (low sodium)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil (extra virgin)
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 2 tablespoons butter (unsalted)
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Serve with:**
- Jasmine rice or brown rice (1 cup dry)
- Steamed broccoli (2 heads)

**Instructions:**
1. Pat salmon dry and season with salt and pepper
2. Mix honey, soy sauce, garlic, and lemon juice in a bowl
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
4. Sear salmon skin-side up for 4 minutes
5. Flip and pour sauce around the fish
6. Add butter and baste for 3-4 minutes until cooked through
7. Garnish with parsley and serve

### Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables
*Prep: 15 min | Cook: 12 min | Serves: 4*

A healthy, colorful stir-fry loaded with fresh vegetables.

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 lbs chicken breast, boneless skinless, cut into strips
- 2 red bell peppers, sliced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 cup snap peas
- 3 carrots, julienned
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Sesame seeds for garnish

**Serve with:**
- Jasmine rice (1.5 cups dry) or rice noodles

**Instructions:**
1. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, and cornstarch for sauce
2. Heat sesame oil in wok over high heat
3. Stir-fry chicken until golden (5 min), set aside
4. Add vegetables, garlic, and ginger; stir-fry 4 minutes
5. Return chicken, add sauce, toss until coated
6. Top with green onions and sesame seeds

### Classic Beef Tacos
*Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 6*

Family favorite taco night made easy.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb ground beef (80/20 lean)
- 1 packet taco seasoning OR homemade:
  - 1 tablespoon chili powder
  - 1 teaspoon cumin
  - 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  - 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  - Salt and pepper to taste
- 12 taco shells (hard) or flour tortillas (soft)
- 1/2 cup water

**Toppings:**
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 cups shredded lettuce
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 lime, cut into wedges
- Salsa (your favorite)

**Instructions:**
1. Brown ground beef in skillet, drain fat
2. Add seasoning and water, simmer 5 minutes
3. Warm taco shells according to package
4. Set up toppings bar and let everyone build their own

## Weekend Specials

### Homemade Pasta with Marinara
*Prep: 20 min | Cook: 25 min | Serves: 4*

Simple comfort food done right.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb spaghetti or penne pasta
- 1 jar marinara sauce (24 oz, no sugar added) OR homemade:
  - 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  - 4 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1/4 cup olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon dried basil
  - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Pinch of red pepper flakes
- 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- Optional: 1 lb Italian sausage or meatballs

**Side:**
- Garlic bread (1 baguette, butter, garlic)
- Side salad (mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook pasta according to package, reserve 1 cup pasta water
2. If making sauce: sauté garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes and seasonings, simmer 20 min
3. Toss pasta with sauce, adding pasta water to loosen
4. Top with fresh basil and parmesan

### Grilled Ribeye Steak
*Prep: 5 min | Rest: 30 min | Cook: 10 min | Serves: 2*

Restaurant-quality steak at home.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 ribeye steaks (12-14 oz each, 1 inch thick)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Coarse salt (kosher or sea salt)
- Fresh cracked black pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- Fresh thyme sprigs

**Sides:**
- Baked potatoes (2 large russet)
- Asparagus (1 bunch, about 1 lb)
- Compound butter (butter + herbs)

**Instructions:**
1. Remove steaks from fridge 30 min before cooking
2. Pat dry and season generously with salt and pepper
3. Preheat grill or cast iron to high heat
4. Cook 4-5 min per side for medium-rare
5. Add butter, rosemary, garlic, thyme and baste
6. Rest 5 minutes before slicing

## Healthy Options

### Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl
*Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min | Serves: 4*

A nutritious, protein-packed bowl.

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 cups quinoa, rinsed
- 3 cups vegetable broth or water
- 1 can chickpeas (15 oz), drained and rinsed
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted
- 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped

**Lemon Herb Dressing:**
- 1/4 cup olive oil (extra virgin)
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Cook quinoa in broth according to package, let cool
2. Whisk dressing ingredients together
3. Combine quinoa with vegetables, chickpeas, olives
4. Drizzle with dressing, top with feta and herbs

### Thai Tofu Curry
*Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min | Serves: 4*

A creamy, flavorful vegan curry.

**Ingredients:**
- 14 oz extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 1 can coconut milk (full fat, 13.5 oz)
- 3 tablespoons red curry paste
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar or maple syrup
- 1 lime, juiced
- Fresh Thai basil or regular basil
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

**Serve with:**
- Jasmine rice (1.5 cups dry)
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges
- Sriracha (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Press tofu for 15 min, cube and pan-fry until golden
2. Sauté curry paste in oil for 1 minute
3. Add coconut milk, soy sauce, sugar; bring to simmer
4. Add vegetables, cook 5 minutes
5. Add tofu and spinach, finish with lime juice
6. Serve over rice with fresh basil

## Breakfast Favorites

### Fluffy Pancakes
*Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 4 (12 pancakes)*

Classic weekend breakfast.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups buttermilk (or milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice)
- 2 large eggs
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

**Toppings:**
- Pure maple syrup
- Fresh blueberries (1 pint)
- Fresh strawberries, sliced (1 pint)
- Whipped cream
- Butter pats

**Instructions:**
1. Whisk dry ingredients in large bowl
2. Whisk wet ingredients separately
3. Combine wet into dry, mix until just combined (lumps OK)
4. Cook on buttered griddle at 350°F until bubbles form
5. Flip and cook 1-2 more minutes

### Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs
*Prep: 5 min | Cook: 10 min | Serves: 2*

Trendy but delicious.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 slices sourdough bread, thick cut
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 large eggs (for poaching)
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar (for poaching water)
- Red pepper flakes
- Flaky sea salt
- Fresh cracked black pepper
- Optional: everything bagel seasoning

**Additions:**
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Microgreens or arugula
- Crumbled feta cheese
- Smoked salmon (lox)

**Instructions:**
1. Toast bread until golden and crispy
2. Mash avocado with salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
3. Poach eggs: simmer water with vinegar, create gentle whirlpool, drop egg, cook 3 min
4. Spread avocado on toast, top with poached egg
5. Season with flaky salt and more red pepper flakes
